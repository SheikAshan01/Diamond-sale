The Most Popular Diamond Shape

The marquise cut diamond is a unique and elegant shape that resembles a boat or an elongated oval with pointed ends. Here are the key specifications and characteristics to consider when evaluating a marquise cut diamond:

Specifications

  • Shape: unique and elegant
  • Cut Quality: Excellent
  • Color: G
  • Clarity: VS2
  • Carat Weight: 1.0 ct

Price

₹3 lakhs to ₹10 lakhs


More Details

The oval cut diamond is a stunning choice for those who love the brilliance of a round cut but desire something more unique. Its elongated shape can make the diamond appear larger than it is, and it often has a slimming effect on the finger. Oval cut diamonds are known for their fire and brilliance, making them a popular choice for engagement rings and other fine jewelry. They are versatile and can be set in a variety of styles, from classic solitaires to intricate vintage designs.
