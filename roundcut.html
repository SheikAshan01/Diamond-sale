The Most Popular Diamond Shape

The round cut diamond is renowned for its brilliance and fire. It is the most popular diamond shape, making up more than 75% of all diamonds sold. Its 58 facets reflect light in a way that maximizes the diamond's sparkle.

More Details

Round cut diamonds are the most researched and sought-after diamonds in the world. Their symmetrical shape and precise facets allow for maximum light reflection, making them the most brilliant of all diamond shapes. They are versatile and can be used in a variety of jewelry settings, from engagement rings to earrings and necklaces.

When choosing a round cut diamond, it's important to consider the 4 Cs: Cut, Color, Clarity, and Carat Weight. The cut is the most crucial factor as it determines how well the diamond reflects light. An excellent cut will ensure that the diamond sparkles brilliantly.
